Daughter

Being a single father has taught me a lot thus far; more than I could ever imagine. My daughter has brought out the best in me, her smile so glimming, her positive outlook in life; dispite all we've been through. She's determined to create a quality of life unmatched by our family. She is fifthteen years young making the best out of life living each day to the fullest. As we acquire a new age, newer territory, so-to-speak, I feel even more obligated to lead by example as she continues uncharted territory. Together, we will succeed, for failure is not an option. "Everyone has failed one time or another in their lifetime, but, you will never become a failure unless you quit"! There is no question going forward. Challenging myself; learning from everyone is so rewarding. We all working together today for a greater tomorrow. Encourage one another, embrace new ideas, or technology, say it, share your thoughts, the time is now as we build the future through light.
